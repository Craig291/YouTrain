YouTrain

Established in 2006, YouTrain is an independent training company delivering IT training throughout Scotland and North West England. We have extensive experience in delivering both Modern Apprenticeship programmes and a wide range of IT technical training courses. We are uniquely placed in the market as Scotland's only Cisco Learning Partner, a Microsoft Silver Learning Partner, an SQA Centre, and a Skills Development Scotland provider.

Our Head Office is based in Stirling, with regional offices in

  • Glasgow
  • Inverness
  • Halifax

Operating across these areas we deliver Technical Training to IT Professionals both through Instructor-led Training Courses and work-based Apprenticeship Programmes.

We also deliver a range of Web development courses as well as IT end-user programmes, predominantly Microsoft Office Specialist (MOS) which can be delivered alongside our Business and Administration apprenticeships. We recognise how important providing a quality service is and our team of excellent, highly experienced and committed employees are dedicated to providing an exceptional service in all areas of our business.

We are a vibrant training company who thrive on supporting and continually expanding our internal expertise. In turn this helps us ensure that we are able to continue to deliver a fully comprehensive training experience to each and every learner.
